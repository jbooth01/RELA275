Suffering

His Suffering

In order to satisfy the demands of justice (a punishment for sin), Jesus went to the Garden of Gethsemane to suffer pain unimaginable. The suffering was so much that Jesus bled from every pore. (Mosiah 3:9)


Not only did Jesus suffer for the sins of all mankind, but he also suffered pains, afflictions, and temptations of every kind. Because it was to be an infinite Atonement, he had to suffer everything we could feel so that he would be able to succor us in our own afflictions. Since Jesus paid the price of sin, we are able to repent of our sins and receive forgiveness and mercy. (Alma 7:11-14)
