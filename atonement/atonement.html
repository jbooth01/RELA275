The Atonement of Jesus Christ

An infinite sacrifice.

The Atonement of Jesus Christ is one of the most important events to ever occur. Because of the fall of Adam and Eve, opposition, and consequently sin, entered into the world. This was not a bad thing, however, because it is through opposition that we are able to grow and progress in this life on earth. However, since we are not perfect, an atonement was needed or else all mankind would be doomed.


The purpose of the Atonement was to satisfy the demands of justice for sins we all commit. With the price of sin paid and the demands of justice satisfied, mercy was allowed to be granted to all of us who use the Atonement to repent of our sins and follow Christ. Jesus was the only person capable of performing the Atonement.


The Atonement consists of three main events: Christ's suffering in the Garden of Gethsemane, Christ's crucifixion on the cross at Calvary, and Christ's resurrection from the dead.


2 Nephi 9:5-24
