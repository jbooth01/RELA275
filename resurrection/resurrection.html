Resurrection

His Resurrection

On the third day after Jesus died, he rose from the tomb and was resurrected from the dead. His body and spirit came together in a perfect state, and because Jesus was resurrected, we will all be resurrected one day as well. The resurrection of Jesus Christ overcame the spiritual death that happened from the fall, and through the resurrection, we can be brought back into God's presence. (Helaman 14:15-17)


Through his suffering, death, and resurrection, Jesus Christ fulfilled the will of the father and completed the infinite Atonement, which redeems all mankind from physical and spiritual death. Because of the Atonement, we are able to repent of our sins and be forgiven. Redemption is free through Jesus Christ, however gaining eternal life is not. We still have to choose to follow Christ and live his gospel. We need to apply the atoning blood of Christ in our lives to receive forgiveness of our sins and have our hearts purified through him. (Mosiah 4:2)
